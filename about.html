About Us

Hack-a-way is a fully-fledged one of a kind beginner-friendly hackathon. Our aim is to provide a platform to create solutions or "hack ways" to real-world problems and let us make a significant change in the world, together.

Prizes will anounce soon....

MISSION AND VISSION

Our mission is to help people adopt a hacking mindset so that they can overcome the problems of our digital era and assure quicker, more sustainable innovation cycles. We envision a hackathon that will create solutions to problems we humans face in real life by applying technologies relating to hardware, software and design. We aim to provide a platform where everyone can hack, find solutions and ultimately "hack a way" into a better future.
